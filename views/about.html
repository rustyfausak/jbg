{{ org.name }} was founded by Jess Fausak in 2025.

In December of 2015, Jess experienced a life-altering event when she suffered a brain injury due to a ruptured aneurysm.

She underwent emergency brain surgery in Austin, TX to place a clip on the aneurysm. After a month long stay in the ICU she returned home to begin the long road of recovery.

Over the years she has learned to navigate life with the challenges of a brain injury. Now she is passionate about helping others who are on similar journeys.
